Our Approach

What is localactors.org?

localactors.org is launched as a social enterprise to help anybody support good cause - non-for-profit - projects and to help those projects to get off the ground.

We believe in local communities and people active in them. They know their needs and can help themselves to care for them: let it be a school coach trip or a new dwell in a village. What they need is a little support in financing, knowledge or materials to get things going.

Global Issues - Local Action

With economic recession all small and locally targeted projects are in danger.

Yet there is a lot of will to keep the local action running! If you have a project, through LocalActors.org you can address small donors directly and world-wide. If you want to support a particular local project, you can choose to do so either financially, with human resources or your skills.

Transparency and feedback

We want to support local action because we see the purpose and the result clearly.

At the same time we want to know what happened next; were the animals saved, was the school build? To join the local action, we all want to know where exactly the support goes and which specific benefits it brings.

The Local Actor

When you work on a local project you are a local actor.

Want you to have access to wide and passionate support. We start as a small group of those not happy with the options opened for small donors - little transparency, no feedback and no visibility of what my contribution has done is most annoying. We also know a few project which are desperately looking for funding - with the economic crisis that now drives them off from public funds as well as from bigger non-governmental organisations (NGOs).

When you support local action with your means you are a local actor.
